lasanga

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 180°C and lightly grease a 20 cm. x 20 cm. square ovenproof lasagne dish.
  • Heat oil in large frying pan over medium heat.
  • Add garlic and zucchini and cook until softened.
  • Add flour and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  • Pour in chicken stock and cook for 5 minutes or until the mixture begins to thicken.
  • Stir in a can and a half of Kraft Cheddar Cheese and ¼ cup of grated parmesan.
  • Add chicken and spinach.
  • Serve and Enjoy!
